Hi, my name is Meike Hatzel and currently I am a PostDoc at the National Institute of Informatics in Tokyo working with Ken-ichi Kawarabayashi.

My main field of research is structure theory on directed graphs. I finished my PhD under the supervision of Stephan Kreutzer at the TU Berlin.

When I am not thinking about graph theory, I enjoy skiing, hiking and making music.
