Hi, my name is Meike Hatzel and currently I am a PostDoc at the IBS in Daejeon. Before I was a PostDoc at the National Institute of Informatics in Tokyo, from summer '22 to summer '24, working with Ken-ichi Kawarabayashi

My main field of research is structure theory on directed graphs but I also interested in other fields of graph theory. I finished my PhD under the supervision of Stephan Kreutzer at the TU Berlin in 2022.

When I am not thinking about graph theory, I enjoy skiing, hiking and making music.
